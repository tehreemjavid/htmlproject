All About Paw Patrol Pups!

What is the Paw Patrol?

The Paw Patrol is a group of search and rescue dogs that's lead by a boy named Ryder.
The Pups all have a special set of skills that allows them to work together to protect Adventure Bay!

Who are the pups?

  • Chase is a police pup
  • Marshall is a fire pup
  • Rubble is a construction pup
  • Rocky is a recycling pup
  • Zuma is an aquatic rescue pup
  • Skye is an aviator pup
